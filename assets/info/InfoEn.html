About the App

CO₂App is an educational tool designed to help users understand how human activities contribute to increasing atmospheric CO₂.

  • Quiz: Test your knowledge on climate change sources.
  • Footprint Calculator: Estimate emissions from housing, transport, and lifestyle in a simple yet reliable way.
